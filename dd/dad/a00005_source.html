8  * Maps the characters to type to the corresponding key codes.
9  *
10  * <p>
11  * These key codes represent the key(s) to be pressed. The
12  * mapping is done with the help of two <code>HashMap</code>s :
13  * <ol>
14  * <li>
15  * <i>Key Map</i> - Maps characters to key codes
16  * where pressing shift key is not required.
17  * </li>
18  * <li>
19  * <i>Shift Key Map</i> - Maps characters to key
20  * codes where pressing shift key is required. For
21  * example, the character <code>%</code> is mapped
22  * to <code>5</code> since <code>Shift+5</code> is
23  * pressed for typing <code>%</code>.
24  * </li>
25  * </ol>
26  * </p>
27  */
